# Mango (container nursery)

**Tropical warm-only** — cold roots stop growth fast. EC **≈1.0–1.6 mS/cm**; watch **anthracnose** when RH stays high on flush.

Juvenile mangoes in pots may fruit **years 3–5+**. Never ship cold-stressed liners into warm zones without acclimation.
