# Rice nutrition (aquaponics / shallow water)

Rice in bench-scale aquaponics or shallow trays runs **low EC** (~0.5–1.0 mS/cm) with **warm roots** and **constant shallow water**. Fish waste often supplies nitrogen — watch for **iron chlorosis** if water is too alkaline.

Assign the rice profile in Start grow or Plants so Guardian cites structured mS/cm targets. Use **Variety / cultivar** for the strain (Basmati, Jasmine, etc.) after picking Rice from the catalog.
