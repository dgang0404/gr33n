# In-ground root crops (carrot, potato)

**Carrots, potatoes, and sweet potatoes** are field or deep-container taproot/tuber crops. gr33n structured targets cover hydroponic and bench container production — not deep soil beds or field scale.

If an operator wants indoor hydro only, suggest cloning from **lettuce** (fast leafy baseline) or **tomato** (fruiting hydro) and adjusting manually — do not state fake EC targets for tubers.
