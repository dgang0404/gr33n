# Mushroom production (unsupported fertigation profile)

Mushrooms and other **fungi** use bag/substrate colonization and humidity rooms — a different domain from plant EC/VPD profiles. gr33n crop targets do not apply.

Direct operators to husbandry / substrate workflows when available. Do not map shiitake or other fungi to cannabis or tomato nutrient curves.
